What is Payout Service?

Simplify Business Payments with Automated Payouts

Payout services are digital platforms that enable businesses to automate, manage, and execute outgoing payments seamlessly. Companies can transfer funds instantly or in bulk to beneficiaries such as vendors, partners, employees, or customers.

With integrated banking networks and payment rails like IMPS, NEFT, and UPI, payout systems ensure fast, secure, and reliable transfers while reducing manual effort and operational delays.

Payout APIs

Simple fund transfer APIs to automate your payouts flow

FUND TRANSFER API

Call Fund transfer API with the unique request id, beneficiary details, mode and amount.

Fund Transfer API Request 

{
  "requestId": "pout202008260002",
  "beneficiaryName": "Demo Company",
  "accountNo": "900900900900",
  "ifscCode": "UTIB0002951",
  "amount": 5000,
  "mode": "IMPS",
  "purpose": "For Invoice 20220200789"
}
